Recipe for Pie

Description

Romanian Cabbage Rolls (Sarmale) are made with sour cabbage stuffed with pork, beef, and little bit of bacon. They’re the best cabbage rolls you will ever have!

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Onion
  • Ground pork
  • Sour cabbage leaves
  • Tomato juice
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Prepare the filling: Heat the oil in a skillet over medium-high. Saute the onions until translucent, then add the rice and toast for 1 minute./li>
  2. Roll the filling: Remove the cabbage leaves from the brine. You can soak them in water beforehand to draw out some of the salt.
  4. Bake the rolls: Cover the pot with a lid or foil. Bake at 375F for 2 hours, then remove the cover and cook for another 1 1/2 – 2 hours. Serve hot with a side of sour cream.
